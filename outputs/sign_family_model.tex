\section*{Sign-Family Hypothesis Model}
This report scores candidate sign pairs as allograph, regional, chronological, modifier, or functional-cluster hypotheses. Scores are heuristic and auditable; they do not change the sign inventory.
\subsection*{Model Features}
\begin{itemize}
\item Neighbor-profile cosine similarity from the crosswalk/neighbor stage.
\item Positional similarity from initial, medial, and terminal proportions.
\item Jensen-Shannon divergence by region, site, type, period, phase, and time metadata.
\item Permutation simulations with seed 1729 and 75 iterations per pair/field.
\item Visual priors only for catalog-reviewed Tier 1 pairs; all other pairs remain visual-pending.
\end{itemize}
